Verification vs. Validation — what question does each answer?

---

- **Verification** confirms the system was built **right** — it meets its
  specified requirements (§2.3.5.9, p.138).
- **Validation** confirms the **right** system was built — it fulfills its
  intended use / stakeholder needs in the operational environment (§2.3.5.11, p.146).
