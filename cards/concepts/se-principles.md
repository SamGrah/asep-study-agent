What characterizes a **systems engineering principle**?

---

An SE principle is a broad **guidance proposition** (not a "how-to") that:
- transcends a particular life-cycle model or stage,
- transcends system types,
- transcends a system context,
- informs a worldview on SE,
- is supported by literature / widely accepted (proven across multiple
  organizations and system types),
- is focused, concise, and clearly worded.

(§1.4.3, p.17)
