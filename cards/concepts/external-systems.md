Distinguish **interfacing**, **interoperating**, and **enabling** systems (all external to the SoI).

---

- **Interfacing:** external systems that **share an interface** (physical,
  material, energy, data/information) with the SoI.
- **Interoperating:** interfacing systems that interface with the SoI **in its
  operational environment to perform a common function** supporting the SoI's
  primary purpose. (SoI + interoperating systems can be seen as a system of systems.)
- **Enabling:** external systems that **facilitate the SoI's life-cycle
  activities** but are not part of the operational environment (e.g.,
  production, logistics support, development systems). Some share an interface; some don't.

(§1.3.3, p.10)
