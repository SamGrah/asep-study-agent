How do **emergent properties** arise, and are they always desirable?

---

They arise from interactions **among system elements** and between the
**system and its environment** — and can be **desirable or undesirable**.

Architecture and design analyze element interactions to reinforce desirable and
prevent undesirable emergence (adding derived requirements when they impact the
SoI).

(§1.3.2, p.9)
