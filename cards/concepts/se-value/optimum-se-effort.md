What is the optimum level of SE effort as a fraction of total project cost?

---

Roughly **10% to 14%** of total project cost. SE effort improves project
success up to an optimum, above which ROI diminishes (unwarranted process).
Correlation of SE effort with project success has been measured as high as ~80%.

(§1.2, p.6)
