Why does early SE matter for life-cycle cost (LCC)? (the DAU 1993 finding)

---

By the time only **~20% of actual costs** have been accrued, **over ~80% of
total LCC has already been committed** (Fig 1.4). It is far less costly to fix
issues identified early — good SE is the means of identifying them early,
reducing technical debt.

(§1.2, p.4)
