What do decision gates typically confirm?

---

That:
- increase in system maturity is within the defined threshold;
- the project deliverables satisfy the **business case**;
- resources are sufficient for the stage and subsequent stages;
- unresolved issues for that stage are addressed; and
- overall **risk** of proceeding forward is **acceptable**.

(§2.1.3, p.29)
