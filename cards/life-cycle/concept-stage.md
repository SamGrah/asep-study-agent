What happens in the **Concept stage**?

---

Can include exploratory research; begins with **recognition of a need** for a
new or modified mission/business capability. Explore the solution space (surveys,
trade-off studies, business/mission analysis) addressing short- and long-range
technical, economic, market, and resource factors. Key outcomes: define the
**problem space**, characterize the **solution space**, identify business/mission
requirements and stakeholder needs, and estimate life-cycle cost, schedule, and
performance — with ongoing risk assessment.

(§2.1.2, p.27)
