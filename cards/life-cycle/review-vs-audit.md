Technical **review** vs. **audit** — what's the difference?

---

- **Technical review:** a series of SE activities at logical transition points
  in the life cycle, by which a project's **progress** is assessed against its
  technical requirements using a mutually agreed-upon set of criteria.
- **Audit:** a detailed review of processes and product-definition information,
  **verifying compliance** with requirements and inspecting products to confirm
  they achieved required attributes / conform to the released configuration.

(per ISO/IEC 24748-8 / IEEE 15288.2; §2.1.4, p.31)
