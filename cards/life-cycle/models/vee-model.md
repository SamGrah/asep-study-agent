What is the **Vee model**, and what do its left and right sides represent?

---

A **sequential** approach (Forsberg & Mooz, 1991) that associates each
development stage with a corresponding testing stage. **Left side = system
definition** (stakeholder requirements → system requirements → architecture →
element definition); **bottom + right side = system realization**. Time/maturity
flow left→right. It highlights **continuous validation** with stakeholders,
defining **verification plans during requirements development**, and continuous
risk/opportunity assessment. (Processes still run concurrently, iteratively, and
recursively at each level — see §2.3.1.2.)
