Describe the **sequential** life-cycle approach (and its strengths).

---

Linear sequential stages with feedback loops but a **single delivery**; each
stage depends on the prior stage's deliverables. Emphasizes documentation
completeness, traceability from requirements, and verification of each
representation. **Strengths:** predictability, stability, repeatability, high
assurance. The **waterfall** model (Royce, 1970) characterizes this approach.

(§2.2.1, p.35)
