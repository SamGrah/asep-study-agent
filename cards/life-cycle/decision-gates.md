What is a **decision gate** and where does it occur?

---

A risk-managing **decision point** at the **beginning and end of each stage**,
ensuring progress is gated by clearly visible criteria — confirming readiness to
proceed and that the stage met its objective. Often framed as "project
milestones," "project reviews," or "milestone reviews." Decisions must be clearly
made and documented against stage entry/exit criteria. (Agile uses a different,
more frequent/less formal cadence and avoids the terms "milestones"/"decision gates.")

(§2.1.3, p.29)
